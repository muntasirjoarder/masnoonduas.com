You Can Do This Too

Three steps, anyone can do

Every dua on this site is free to download. You do not need to wait for us — print as many copies as you like and distribute them wherever they will be seen.

01
Print

Download any dua as a print-ready PDF and print it at home or at a local print shop. A4 size, clear Arabic, correct margins — it comes out ready to use.

Browse duas →
02
Laminate

A quick laminate makes the card last for years. Most print shops do this for a few pence. It withstands kitchens, bathrooms, little hands, and time.

03
Distribute

Give them to friends, family, neighbours. Place them in your local mosque, school, or community centre. No permission from us needed — just the intention.

Why We Do This

Putting duas
where people gather

Downloading and printing at home is easy for many families — but not everyone has access to a printer, laminator, or the time to set it up. Mosques, Islamic centres, and madrasahs often have the reach but not always the resources to source and verify authentic printed material.

So we bridge that gap. We print physical sets of dua cards and deliver them for free — for the entrance of a mosque, the walls of a prayer hall, the corner of a madrasah classroom. The intention is purely sadaqah jariyah: a continuous charity whose reward flows as long as someone reads the dua and remembers Allah's name in that moment.

The Prophet ﷺ said: "When a person dies, his deeds come to an end except for three: sadaqah jariyah, knowledge from which others benefit, or a righteous child who prays for him." (Muslim 1631)

Every card placed on a mosque wall is an attempt at exactly that. We ask Allah to accept it from us and from every person who made it possible.
